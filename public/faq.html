Frequently Asked Questions

These frequently asked questions cover service areas, quotes, pricing, accepted items, photos, scheduling, and junk pickup across Southeast Michigan.

Quick answers about quotes, pickup minimums, load sizes, photos, and what to expect when Pure Mitten Junk Removal comes out.

Ask for a Quote Call/Text 734-480-8190

What areas do you serve?

We serve Southeast Michigan, including Detroit, Ann Arbor, Warren, Sterling Heights, Dearborn, Livonia, Troy, Westland, Farmington Hills, Southfield, Rochester Hills, Taylor, Royal Oak, Novi, Pontiac, Canton, Ypsilanti, Monroe, Brighton, Port Huron, and more. If you are close to these areas, send us your address with your estimate request and we will let you know if we can help.

How do I get an estimate?

You can call, text, or use the fast estimate form. No appointment is required and there is no commitment. Photos help us provide an estimated price range based on likely load size, weight, access, and labor. The final exact price is confirmed onsite.

What is the difference between a fast estimate and an on-site quote?

A fast estimate uses your description and photos to provide a price range without an appointment. A free on-site quote gives you an exact price in person. If you approve that price, we can usually remove everything during the same appointment.

Do you have a minimum pickup?

Yes. We do not haul anything under the minimum pickup price because even one small item can still include travel, labor, and dump fees. The minimum pickup helps keep small jobs fair and clear up front.

What can you haul away?

We remove furniture, appliances, garage clutter, basement junk, estate cleanout items, construction debris, yard waste, and many bulky household items. If you are not sure whether we can take something, ask before booking.

Do I need to move everything outside?

No. Full-service junk removal means we can remove items from garages, basements, rooms, offices, and outdoor areas. Please let us know if items are upstairs, downstairs, very heavy, or hard to access.

Can you do same-day or next-day pickups?

Often, yes. Availability depends on the schedule and the size of the job. You can view available pickup windows online or call or text 734-480-8190 with your city, photos, and what needs to go.

Do you donate or recycle items?

When items are in reusable condition and donation or recycling is practical, we do our best to keep them out of the landfill. Some items still have to go to the dump depending on condition and local disposal rules.

How should I prepare before pickup?

Please point out everything that needs to go, clear a walking path if possible, and let us know about any parking rules, stairs, elevators, gates, or tight spaces. We handle the lifting and loading from there.
